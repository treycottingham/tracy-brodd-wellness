About Me

Wellness coaching initially evolved out of need to help my children who were suffering with ADD and eczema. Conventional methods were not helping them, so I began searching for answers on my own. Through trial and error, I found the Chek Institute in 2013 and began taking courses in Holistic Lifestyle Coaching. What began as a way to find some answers for my family has since turned into a passion for overall health and wellness. I discovered that nutrition was a great start, but how you truly take care of your body through proper movement, hydration, relaxation, beneficial thoughts and sleep are very powerful tools for true health and longevity.
